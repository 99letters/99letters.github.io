About

Born from
the lake.

Origin

BIWAKO SILENCE

Lake Biwa has held this land in stillness for four million years. Its shores have shaped not only the geography of Shiga, but the temperament of its people — patient, unhurried, precise.

琵琶湖は四百万年、この土地を静けさの中に包んできた。
その岸辺は、滋賀の人々の気質を形づくった——
忍耐強く、急がず、精緻に。

Mission

Experience
Translation

Shiga Craft exists to translate the experience of silence into objects. Not to explain craft, but to let it speak. Each piece is a conversation between the maker and the material — and eventually, between the object and the one who holds it.

沈黙の体験を、物に翻訳する。
工芸を説明するのではなく、語らせる。
それが、わたしたちの仕事。

Approach

One Material.
One Life.

We work only with artisans who have devoted themselves entirely to a single craft. No generalists. No shortcuts. The objects we present are the result of decades of repetition — of failure, adjustment, and silent perseverance.

ひとつの素材に、生涯を捧げた職人とだけ働く。
ここにあるのは、何十年もの反復の結果——
失敗と修正と、沈黙の積み重ね。
